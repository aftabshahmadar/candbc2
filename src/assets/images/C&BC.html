WHO WE ARE?

47

YEARS OF
EXCELLENCE

Way back, in the year 1977, a small venture had started its manufacturing activities in the name of "Pedhadia Casting" by pioneering efforts of Lt. Mr. Nathabhai Makkanbhai Pedhadia. "Cast and Blower Co. (Guj.) Pvt. Ltd." was founded in the year 1980 by Lt. Mr. Laljibhai Nathabhai Pedhadia with engineering skill, has pioneered it as one of the most reputed quality manufacturers and exporters of all type of castings.

KNOW MORE

Focus : C&BC vision is to be the most innovative and versatile foundry space in the world by catering to all segments of industry where we can offer cast iron and ductile iron as the best applicable material to customer need. We will grow equally to all segments to keep us afloat to any kind of global economic situation.

most innovative and versatile foundry

OUR VISION

Global : World is changing fast, requirements are changing. Product base material is changing. We see Cast Iron today as secondary preferred material against plastic and other light metal. Thus adopting the best process and system to make cast iron and ductile iron preferred material for the components by investing on innovation.

Growth : C&BC will continue to invest in people, processes, and technology to ensure that we remain at the forefront of the industry. We will be a responsible corporate citizen, committed to sustainability and environmental stewardship.

high quality cast iron products and services

OUR MISSION

Preferred supplier : C&BC mission is to be the leading provider of high quality cast iron products and services. We will use our expertise and experience to create innovative solutions that meet the needs of our customers. We will continuously strive to improve our processes and products to ensure that our customer receive the best possible value for their money. We will be committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for our employees and to being a responsible corporate citizen in our community

Development : C&BC mission to be recognized for commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer service. We will strive to work with shortest lead time for new product development.

Strive to help : C&BC mission is to be partner to its customer and supplier. It will stand by all its partner as strong pillar against any odds. C&BC will step up to it customer needs at all time and help its supplier to step up to C&BC requirements. We will work hand in hand with our partners for mutual growth. C&BC will stand by its employee and implement policy to ensure a secured future.

our company journey

JOURNEY SO FAR

  • 1977

    Way back in 1977, a small venture was started by the Lt. Mr. Nathabhai Pedhadia.

  • 1980

    C&BC was founded by the Lt. Laljibhai Pedhadia, with engineering skills to produce pumps for the agriculture, domestic, and industrial markets.

  • 1994

    Started manufacturing engine-driven centrifugal pumps.

  • 2001

    C&BC received the prestigious National Award for best quality products in 2001.

  • 2001

    Began producing electrical pumps like submersible, openwell, and monoblock pumps.

  • 2005

    Installed Jolt Squeeze Line Arpa 300 and Arpa 450.

  • 2015

    C&BC installed a DISAMATIC high-pressure molding line to produce more than 1,500 tons of castings per month.

  • 2017

    C&BC started manufacturing cast iron drains and fittings.

  • 2019

    C&BC began manufacturing cast iron pipes.

  • 2019

    A subsidiary of C&BC launched a hi-tech foundry plant. The subsidiary's name is Roll and Cast Technologies Pvt. Ltd., with the brand name ROCA.

  • 2020

    Started a new product line with innovative designs in the cast iron cookware range with 40+ SKUs.

Our Company Policy

OUR POLICY

To ensure satisfaction of customer to supply of components as per market need with best quality.

To ensure that supplier works in mutual partnership and helping them to improve where we can to ensure their healthiest survival.

To ensure that we keep the cleanliness of the Premises and have gardening maintained to save the environment.

We commit ourselves to be a part of constructive global growth & thus we will not produce components for any defense equipment's.

SUSTAINABLE FUTURE
